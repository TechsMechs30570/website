About Our Team

We are a group of passionate middle schoolers on a mission to learn robotics, build awesome robots, and compete with other teams. We are based in Flower Mound, Texas, and our team, TechsMechs, was formed this year (2025). Follow our journey as we design, build, and program our robots!

Outreach

On July 18th, 2025 we went to a Hilton Hotels Corporate Office in Dallas for Hilton kids day where we presented the basics of FTC to over 200 parents and children. We also demoed our robot and let the kids who came for the Hilton kids day event to test drive our robot. We made pamphlets with information about FLL (first Lego League), FTC, and our Team. We all had a wonderful time for our first Outreach presentation for the season, and we are looking forward to doing many more.
